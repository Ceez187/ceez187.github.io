What We Do

DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES

From a single check-engine light to full fleet scanning, we handle it on-site.

Check Engine Diagnostic

Full code read, live data, freeze frame, and system analysis. We tell you what's actually wrong — not just a code.

Marketplace Pre-Buy Inspections

Buying off Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, OfferUp, or a private seller? Don't get stuck with someone else's problem. Full scan, module history, readiness checks, and hidden fault detection — before you hand over the cash.

Battery & BMS Reset

Battery registration and BMS reset after replacement. Required on BMW, Mercedes, Audi, VW, domestics, and more.

TPMS Relearn

Sensor programming and relearn after tire service or sensor replacement. Most makes, most protocols.

Fleet Diagnostics — Avoid Downtime

Every hour a work truck sits is money you're not making. We come to your yard and scan, diagnose, and report on-site so your fleet stays moving. Volume pricing and recurring contracts available.

Bi-Directional Component Testing

Command actuators directly — fans, fuel pumps, injectors, EGR, solenoids, cooling. Confirms whether the part is bad or the wiring is, before you spend a dime on parts.

Live Data & Sensor Analysis

Real-time sensor monitoring while the engine runs — fuel trims, O₂ readings, MAF flow, MAP voltage. Catches intermittent faults that a basic code read can't.

Second Opinion Diagnostics

Got a quote you want to verify? We come out, run an independent scan, and give you a clear, written read on what's actually going on. Walk into your next service appointment knowing exactly what you need.

Our Process

HOW WE DIAGNOSE.

A repeatable, proven process — not guesswork. Here's how every job runs.

01

Listen

Every diagnosis starts with what you've noticed — sounds, smells, behavior, when it happens. Symptoms are clues codes won't give you.

02

Scan

Full multi-module scan and live data capture. We see what your vehicle is reporting across every system, not just the engine.

03

Verify

Component-level testing to confirm the actual fault — not the symptom. This is the step most shops skip.

04

Report

Clear written findings, root cause, and recommended next steps. You leave knowing exactly what's wrong and what it'll take to fix it.
